Description

Have you ever needed quick second opinions about anything? What if it can also be fun and anonymous?

LOOK is a fast, fun, and snappy way to discover new looks, share and be yourself. Everything stays anonymous, including you, your photos and your votes.

Simply capture, share, decide.

Features

  • Beautiful fullscreen photos.
  • Portrait and Landscape photo surfing.
  • A simple toggle displays color-coded Like vs Dislike on your looks.
  • Screen tap to show/hide photo overlay actions.
  • Latest native Android Jelly Bean look & feel.

FAQ

Why 'Anonymous'? How does it work?
  • Well. I believe identity is an option. Not everything online has to be attached to a username or avatar. Everyone has a choice to reveal who they are or not.
  • Currently you can put your id in the photo caption, in the future there will be toggle to show/hide your id when posting.
  • Commenting is being implemented, the idea is each comment will be numbered from 1 to n, to refer to another comment type '@comment_number' so '@10' as example. I'm still working this out so feel free to email me your tips.
Why 'Beta'? When is the official release?
Beta because the idea of 'anonymous' social sharing is quite different with most networks out there so it's being tested. I am learning and building this app from scratch, by myself, that includes UI/UX, front and back ends. So if you'd like to give me a hand that'd be great. My plan for official release is Feb 14, 2014 ;) Will see how it goes.
What is the backing for?
Midnight coffee + Server cost. I am bootstrapping this with shared-hosting right now, it runs great so far for the current demand, but you know it's not gonna hold for long. There is a Paypal donation button/link built-in the app so that can be used as well. Thanks for everything, from: reading this, checking out the app, talking about it, sharing, and backing.
